# Sample Rosette Paper

We propose a simple dynamical model for OAK-safe extraction. We show that a minimal state equation can be used as a reproducibility seed.

The core equation is:

$$
\frac{dx}{dt} = -k x + u(t)
$$

Assume k > 0 and bounded input u(t). However, this sample has no real figure data and therefore cannot certify reproduction.
