
### 2.3 OAKGate

Proof, measurement, risk, uncertainty, reversibility, and qualified human requirement.

The living body comes before theory.


The agent chooses its autonomy level according to danger:

```text
ZERO-TOUCH  = safe routine creation
DRY-RUN     = risky simulation
ONE-TOUCH   = sensitive commitment
NO-TOUCH    = medical / emergency / irreversible / destructive
```

No leakage of medical, personal, legal, financial, security, or patent-sensitive data.

### 2.7 Human Sovereignty Layer

Qualified humans remain required for medical, legal, financial, emergency, irreversible, and high-stakes decisions.
