```text
simple, direct, adaptive, proof-oriented, attractive, respectful, alive.
```

## 1. Voice formula

```text
JKD + OAK + 8e Feu = public voice
```

Meaning:

- JKD gives directness and simplicity ;
- OAK gives proof and rigor ;
- 8e Feu gives responsibility, Gaia and living-system ethics.

## 2. Tone

Use:

```text
short lines
strong verbs
clear mission
visible proof
respectful limits
no useless jargon
no empty grandiosity
```

Avoid:

```text
inflated claims
unverified certainty
cultural decoration
technical fog
messianic tone
promises of guaranteed success
```

## 7. Proof discipline

Every public claim should be tagged mentally:

```text
vision
hypothesis
prototype
test
measurement
proof
product
company
```

Do not let vision pretend to be proof.
Do not let prototype pretend to be market validation.
Do not let market hypothesis pretend to be revenue.
