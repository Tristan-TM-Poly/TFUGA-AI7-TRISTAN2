## OAK labels

```text
Vision: inspiring direction, not evidence.
Doctrine: structured model, not execution.
Prototype: executable or concrete artifact.
Tested: has automated or reproducible checks.
Merged: integrated into main branch.
Product Candidate: can become an offer.
Validated Offer: real external demand or sale.
Company: legal/business structure with operations.
```
