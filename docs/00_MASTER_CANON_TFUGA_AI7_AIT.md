## 4. Minimum canon cell

Every canon cell must contain:

```yaml
id: stable identifier
title: short name
domain: math | physics | ai | ait | prototype | governance | climate | energy | cognition
status: OAK status
claim: what is being asserted
objects: formal objects involved
operators: transformations or algorithms
hypotheses: explicit assumptions
equations: main equations
verification: proof, test, simulation, benchmark, measurement, or none
risks: strongest known risks and failure modes
next_test: smallest useful next verification step
links: related files, repos, papers, datasets, prototypes
```
