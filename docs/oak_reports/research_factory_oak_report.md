# OAK Report — Ω-AIT-RESEARCH-FACTORY-T

## Scope

ResearchFactory turns CanonOS branches into reviewed plans for experiments, prototypes, benchmarks, reusable assets, and safe next actions.

It does not publish, merge, deploy, send messages, make commitments, or perform external actions.

## OAK status

- **Observe:** classify hypothesis, benchmark level, value score, release status, and factory line.
- **Act safely:** produce experiment plans, benchmark targets, review packets, and draft-PR-ready artifacts.
- **Kill-risk:** hold, quarantine, or require review for sensitive, public-facing, high-risk, or insufficiently tested branches.

## Core boundaries

1. No prototype claim without tests.
2. No strong performance claim without benchmark.
3. No public-facing release without review gates.
4. No value hypothesis presented as scientific proof.
5. Failed tests become M− and next iteration.
6. Factory outputs are plans, not permission for external action.
7. No auto-merge and no auto-publish.

## Review checklist

- ExperimentEngine requires baseline, metric, success condition, and failure expectation.
- BenchmarkLadder remains a status label, not proof.
- ValueGate is planning only.
- ReleaseGate holds unclear or sensitive material.
- FactoryScheduler routes weak ideas back to RealityAnchor or experiments.
- ProofToAssetCompiler generates paths/plans only.

## Final law

```math
\boxed{\text{CanonOS gouverne le savoir; ResearchFactory transforme le savoir en plans de preuve, prototypes, benchmarks et actifs réutilisables.}}
```
